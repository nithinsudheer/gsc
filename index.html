Platform Features

Everything Designed for Creators

The only Indian marketplace with escrow payments, GST invoicing, KYC verification, and instant UPI payouts, all in one platform.

Instant UPI Payouts

Once your go-live proof is submitted and validated, escrow is released directly to your bank account via UPI. No 30-day waits, no chasing invoices.

Two Verification Badges

Earn a KYC Verified badge (Aadhaar and PAN review) and a Social Verified badge (YouTube stats pulled directly via API). Both are shown on your profile and your proposals — brands see them before they hire.

GST-Compliant Invoicing

Every collaboration auto-generates two GST-compliant invoices — one for you, one for the brand. No spreadsheets, no manual paperwork, no chasing the brand for a receipt.

In-Platform Collaboration

All briefs, feedback, file sharing, and content submissions happen inside the platform. Messages, documents, and revision history are timestamped and available as evidence if a dispute arises.

Creator Score Badges

Every completed collaboration builds your Creator Score. Earn badges like Rising Star, On Time, Reliable, and Top Rated — visible to every brand who views your profile or receives your proposal.

Direct Brand Discovery

Brands search by niche, platform, location, follower tier, and language. When your profile matches, brands find you and book directly — no cold DMs, no middlemen.
